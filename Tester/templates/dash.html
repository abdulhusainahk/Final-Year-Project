The website Quest on Marks is the result of our project Semantic Text Matching for Question Answer System. It is a project to develop a question answer matching system for evaluation of examination answers of students.

The system will semantically match the student's answer for given questions and give the validity of the answer in the form of score. Higher the matching degree between the question and the answer, more will be the score for that answer.
